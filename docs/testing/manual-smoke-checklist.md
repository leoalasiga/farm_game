# Pixel Farm Adventure Manual Smoke Checklist

- Start a new game on the farm
- Till one empty plot
- Plant one radish
- Water the planted radish
- Sleep to the next day with `N`
- Harvest the ready radish
- Enter the village from the farm gate with `E`
- Sell the harvested radish with `S`
- Buy one radish seed with `B`
- Unlock the forest after completing the onboarding quest flow
- Enter the forest and gather wood
- Gather enough wood and stone to unlock the mine at the forge with `U`
- Enter the mine and gather copper ore
- Upgrade the axe at the forge with `U`
- Save the game with `K`
